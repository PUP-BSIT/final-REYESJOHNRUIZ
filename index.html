HOW DO I SEE MYSELF 5 YEARS FROM NOW?

5 years from now, I see myself having a job where I can say that all of my hardships in college were worth it.

I can see myself capable of helping and giving back to my parents and to the people who helped me to get through my college. In this time, I can also provide enough food for myself, my siblings and my pets.

In terms of work: I can see myself learning new things, adapting to ever evolving technology and contributing to a good and healthy environment around me. I am a good programmer and designer, 5 years from now.

These past few months have been really hard and stressful not only because of the academics but also because of my personal problems in life.

Academics have been hard for me because I don't have my own laptop, I was only borrowing it. There are times that I can't go to school because of financial problems and there are also times wherein I would find a place to stay for the night because my father does not want me to be in the house.

College was hard and my personal problems are making it harder. But I have a dream, I have a goal that I badly want to achieve. I want to have a house that I can call "home" and this education, this university and this course would serve as a bridge towards reaching my goals. Therefore, yes it is worth and I still want to be here, no matter how hard it would become.
