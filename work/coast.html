Exploring Coast Guard

What is Coast Guard?

The Coast Guard is a maritime military service responsible for enforcing maritime law, conducting search and rescue operations, and ensuring maritime safety and security.

Roles in Business Management

Roles include commissioned officers, petty officers, and rescue swimmers. Each role requires specialized training and maritime expertise.

Skills Needed

Successful Coast Guard members need strong teamwork, leadership, and physical fitness, along with proficiency in maritime operations and navigation.
